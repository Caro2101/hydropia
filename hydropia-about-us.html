About us

The creation of Hydropia

A new way to produce vegetables

Hydropia is a company specialized in hydroponics. It was created in 2020. It is located on the outskirts of Lille and has 3 hydroponic greenhouses equipped with the latest technology. Our team contains specialists in Hydroponics who will aim to give the best quality products.

We deliver throughout France, Belgium and the Netherlands.

Our Vision

Revolutionate the agriculture industry

We want to breathe new life into agriculture by making it more responsible, easier and more sustainable. By using the Hydroponics technique, we produce healthy products in a more efficient way. Our second goal is to generalize this method, which requires little space and few resources.

Finally, with Hydropia, we want to expand throughout Europe, starting with Germany. The aim is to have different greenhouses in different countries to give a more efficient and responsible production and delivery.
